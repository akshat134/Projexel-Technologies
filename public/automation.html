Servos/Stepper

One of the most important elements used in automation. Steppers are an Open Loop system, meaning that they directly proceed progressive pulses. Servos are closed-loop systems, meaning they use an encoder, or resolver, to give reponse of the real position vs the control position, the servo then adjusts the current to command the motor location.
