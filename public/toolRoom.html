featuresProducts Features

Jig

  • Holds a piece of work and guides the tool operating on it
  • Hstens repetitive hole center location on multiple interchangeable parts
  • Used to test faults in Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs)
  • Used for holding work and helping to shape jewellery components made out of wire or small sheets of metal
  • Used to make finish stringers, in interior staircase fabrication and for sharpening woodworking tools

Gauge

  • To pull or push for gap width adjustments
  • Unique hole allows fall protection and wedge extraction
  • Allows scraping, poking, and prodding post weld to remove slag, porosity and identify defects
  • Used to measure landings quickly and accurately
  • Efficient and easy to use

Fixture

  • Used to securely locate (position in a specific location or orientation) and support the work
  • Using a fixture improves the economy of production by allowing smooth operation and quick transition from part to part
  • Helps to create a secure mounting point for a workpiece, allowing for support during operation and increased accuracy and precision
  • Help in reducing labor costs and also used for drilling operations

SPM

  • Jig boring machine
  • Co-ordinate drilling and boring machine
  • Rigid die sinking machine Center lathe with hydro copying attachment
  • Rotary surface grinding Cylindrical grinder
  • Optical profile grinding machine
